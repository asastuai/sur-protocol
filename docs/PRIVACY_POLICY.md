# Privacy Policy

**SUR Protocol — Privacy Policy**
Last updated: March 15, 2026

## 1. Introduction

SUR Protocol ("we", "us", "our") operates a decentralized perpetual futures trading platform on Base L2. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our platform.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Blockchain Data
- Public wallet addresses used to interact with our smart contracts
- On-chain transaction data (deposits, withdrawals, trades)
- This data is inherently public on the blockchain

### 2.2 Technical Data
- IP address and approximate geolocation
- Browser type, device type, and operating system
- Pages visited and features used
- WebSocket connection metadata

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect
- Personal identification (name, email, phone) — we do not require account registration
- KYC/AML documentation (unless required by applicable law)
- Private keys or seed phrases — we never have access to these

## 3. How We Use Information

- To provide and maintain the trading platform
- To monitor system performance and prevent abuse
- To improve user experience and develop new features
- To comply with legal obligations

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:
- Blockchain networks (transaction data is public by nature)
- Infrastructure providers (hosting, analytics) under strict data processing agreements
- Law enforcement when required by valid legal process

## 5. Data Retention

- On-chain data is permanent and immutable by nature of blockchain technology
- Off-chain technical logs are retained for up to 90 days
- You may request deletion of off-chain data by contacting us

## 6. Cookies

We use minimal cookies for:
- Session management
- User preferences (language, theme)
- We do not use third-party tracking cookies

## 7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- TLS encryption for all connections
- Smart contract audits
- Regular security assessments

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request correction or deletion
- Object to processing
- Data portability

## 9. International Users

SUR Protocol is accessible globally. By using the platform, you consent to the transfer of data to the jurisdictions where our infrastructure operates.

## 10. Changes

We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be announced through the platform.

For privacy-related inquiries: privacy@sur.exchange
